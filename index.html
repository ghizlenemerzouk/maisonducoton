Our Story

Maison du Coton was founded in 1995 with a simple mission: to provide the highest quality cotton products for the modern home. We source our materials directly from sustainable farms and employ traditional craftsmanship techniques.

Every product in our collection is made from 100% Egyptian cotton, known for its extra-long fibers that create a softer, more durable fabric.
